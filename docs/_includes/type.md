## Typography
---

Based has a default font scale of `1.5` derived from the font-size value on your app's `:root` selector. So assuming you left that at `16px`, scaling looks something like this:

|  Class   |   Scale    | Size
| :--------| :--------- | :-----|
| `.h1`    |  5.0625rem | 81px
| `.h2`    |  3.375rem  | 54px
| `.h3`    |  2.25rem   | 36px
| `.h4`    |  1.5rem    | 24px
| `.h5`    |  1rem      | 16px
| `.p`     |  1rem      | 16px
| `.sm`    |  0.8125rem | 13px
